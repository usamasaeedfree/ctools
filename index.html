Step By Step Guide To Use This Tool

  1. Enter your subject name (it's optional).
  2. Select your grade from the grades list menu.
  3. Enter the credit hours of that subject.
  4. Click on the "Add Subject" button and add unlimited subjects as you need.
  5. After inputting all details, click on "Calculate CGPA."
  6. In the Result Section, you can check your CGPA and performance status.
  7. If you want to remove any subject, you can remove it by clicking on the Remove button.

Understanding Your CGPA Result

When you click on the "Calculate CGPA" button, then your result will be displayed in the large number form. Along with this, you can also get a performance label.
